PIW - Cisco Learning Spaces | Boosting student engagement with collaboration
With a Cisco global addressable market of $8.9 billion, the opportunities for partners to drive educational impact through technology are many. As education leaders are under pressure to improve student engagement and student success, they are looking for solutions that can deliver new learning models that are unconstrained by the traditional classroom. In today’s competitive climate, students expect and demand a personalized experience that allows them to learn anywhere, any time, and on any device.
In this webinar, Cisco Partners will learn how Cisco is meeting these challenges with its Learning Spaces offer, which delivers Cisco’s collaboration portfolio to help create new learning opportunities for students regardless of age, location and where they are in their learning journey.
